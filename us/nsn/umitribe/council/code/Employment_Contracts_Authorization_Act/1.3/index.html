1.3 Findings.

The Council hereby finds and declares that:

(a) On July 22, 2023, the citizens of the Upper Mattaponi Tribe ("Tribe") voted to adopt a new constitution ("Constitution") to assert the Tribe's sovereign authority as a federally-recognized Indian tribe and to reorganize the Tribe's government to exercise its full authority in a fair and equitable manner; and,

(b) The Constitution establishes an executive branch of government comprised of the Chief, Assistant Chief, and various Departments and programs; and,

(c) The Department of Finance, the Department of Records Management, and other key Departments serve critical needs in the operation of the Tribe's government, and employing well-qualified individuals as Directors of these Departments will help ensure the efficient and professional delivery of services; and,

(d) In order to retain well-qualified individuals to serve in these critical positions, the Tribe must offer enforceable employment contracts in order to attract experienced professionals to work for the Tribe; and,

(e) The Council has determined that it is in the best interests of the Tribe to authorize the Chief to enter into employment contracts with Directors of key Departments in the Tribe, and to authorize a limited waiver of sovereign immunity to allow for the enforcement of the employment contracts in the Tribe's Judicial Branch of government.
